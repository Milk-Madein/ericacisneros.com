Erica Cisneros

Creating an artist statement for my own work is messy and rough. The foundation is uneven on the plate of the idea, yet the passion as I have to make the idea a reality is what forces the outcome to be what I want. As the letters form into words, so does my hand moving between pencil to keyboard. From the start the idea does not stay fixed. I have to move my thoughts from one variation to another so I can smooth out the faults but strengthen the results. Pouring over details that make no sense in the process helps me tremendously. I take a speck and make it a glob of nonsense that will only give me joy. A joke or a mental disconnect from the fear of not being able to live up to my own expectations. The speck becomes more of a crutch to comfort myself in the journey. I fear the unknown of my own process. Fear of not doing the norm. Fear turns into curiosity that pulls me out of the arms of the crutch and head first into the unknown. Though fear pulls me along the path I have a sense of euphoria as I see the idea I wanted to make at the start stare at me. The mound that formed on the plate of the idea gets chipped away. Slicing off true piles of nonsense. Then smoothing out all the faults. I end up with a piece that is above what I thought I was able to create.

Description

The aim is to create a game that evokes nostalgia for old tech games, which may have been lost due to rapid technological advancements. Step into the enchanting world of 'Smalltop Circus'! Join Ricky, the lovable clown rat, as he goes up against the mysterious Peculiar Puppeteer, Malachi Mischief, who threatens to overshadow the Smalltop! Unleash Ricky's playful moves, solve puzzles, and experience the magic of the smallest big top. In all new HD Textures that will feel like a childhood memory! Will Ricky remain the star, or will Malachi's mischief steal the spotlight? Get ready for a comical adventure that blends humor, heart, and circus magic in this must-play game for all ages!
